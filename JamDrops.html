Jam Drops

thumbprint cookies filled with raspberry jam.

Ingredients

  • 115 grams (1/2 cup / 1 stick) unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 100 grams (1/2 cup) caster sugar or granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 175 grams (1 and 1/4 cups) plain flour or all purpose flour

    • Buttery Jam Drop Cookies made from scratch. These gorgeous thumbprint cookies are soft and chewy with warm jam baked into the middle. They’re easy to make and perfect for Christmas and holiday gift-giving.Also known as thumbprint cookies, these cookies are quick, easy, and perfect for the holidays. Made with only a handful of everyday ingredients, they’re a great treat to throw together last minute.
